## 7. Crop Support & Harvest

### 1. Crop Support

- **Materials & Tools**: Bamboo poles, netting, clips/ties, support rope.
- **Methodology**: Supporting sprawling plants like tomatoes and cucumbers encourages vertical growth. Options like bamboo, netting, clips, and ties keep plants in place, leading to healthier growth and easier harvesting.
- **Transformation**: Converts vulnerable, unsupported sprawling plants into structured, vertical high-yield crops.

### 2. Harvest

- **Equipment**: Safety gloves, pliers, harvest baskets, polythene bags.
- **Best Practices**: Harvesting crops at their peak ripeness ensures the best flavor and nutritional value. Regular harvesting encourages continued production. Use proper tools and techniques to avoid damaging plants and maximize yield.
- **Standard**: **Quality Produce Market-Ready Standards**

## 8. Carbon Credit Program

**Earn extra income by farming sustainably**

Good farming already saves carbon. Agaate helps you measure, verify, and monetise it — turning sustainable practices into a brand-new income stream, with no extra land required.

### What Are Carbon Credits?

- Every tonne of $\text{CO}_2$ you reduce or store earns **1 verified credit**.
- Credits are purchased by corporations and institutions offsetting their emissions.
- Provides an **additional, regular income stream** on top of your standard crop sales.
- Directly rewards the climate-friendly farming practices you already perform.

### How Agaate Helps You Earn

1. Simple farm enrolment & registration.
2. Practice tracking via the mobile app and periodic field visits.
3. **End-to-End MRV (Measure, Report, Verify)** handled completely for you.
4. Transparent, direct payouts on every verified credit.

### Practices That Qualify

| Sustainable Practice          | Environmental Benefit                                           |
| :---------------------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Reduced & Zero Tillage**    | Keeps carbon locked directly in the soil structure              |
| **Efficient Drip Irrigation** | Conserves water and reduces pumping energy usage                |
| **Organic & Bio-Inputs**      | Significantly cuts chemical nitrogen greenhouse emissions       |
| **No Residue Burning**        | Ensures crop residue is returned cleanly to fertilize the field |
| **Cover Cropping**            | Builds long-term organic soil carbon and prevents erosion       |
| **Better Input Efficiency**   | Lowers input costs while reducing overall carbon footprint      |
